Who Is Victoria Villaroel?
The Woman Rawayana Wrote Its
New Song 'Veneka' About

In recent days, Venezuelan music has made
waves again thanks to "Veneka," the latest
track by the band Rawayana in collaboration
with Akapellah. Although the song had not
been officially released, it went viral on
social media, prompting the band to officially
launch it today, October 21.

Rawayana, Neutro Shorty and
Orestes Gomez present
New Song 'Incomprendido

The band Rawayana continues to amaze us with
their musical versatility by releasing in collaboration
with Amazon Music Original "Incomprendido" their
interpreation originally performed by Ismael Rivera
and Written by Bobby Capo. The band was joint
by the talented venezuelan trapper Neutro Shorty
and the distinguished Orestes Gomez.

Rawayana and friends:
Tiny Desk Concert

This Latinx Heritage Month Tiny Desk is
celebrating with an 'El Tiny' takeover, featuring
a wide array of artists from all corners of Latinidad.
No Venezuelan party is complete without tequeños.
Whether it's a wedding or a baptism, the
cheese-filled treats are a must-have at every

pulled up to "El Tiny" with a box to share.
