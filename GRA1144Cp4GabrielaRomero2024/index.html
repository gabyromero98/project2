History in every corner

Since its opening in 1875, the Palais Garnier has hosted countless
ballets, operas, and concerts. Even today, it continues to be a
significant cultural institution in France. It's a place where you can
connect with Parisian history and appreciate the artistic traditions
that have thrived here for centuries.
